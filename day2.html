Cam

The image sensor is a crucial component of digital cameras that converts light into electrical signals. It comes in various types, such as CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). The sensor's resolution, measured in megapixels, determines the level of detail captured in an image.
